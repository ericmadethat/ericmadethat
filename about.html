Who's this guy?

I'm Eric Nguyen, a web developer, designer, and musician.

I once took a brain test that determined both my left and right brains were equally dominant. This means I enjoy creative expression just as much as analytical thinking. Pretty handy for problem solving!

I originally graduated with a degree in Architecture, but it wasn't really my cup of tea. So I learned graphic design and coding. I worked as a lead designer and developer for a number of companies. I'll let you read the details on LinkedIn.

In 2017, I quit my job to take a gap year and pursue dream projects, travel, and learn new skills. I'm currently in Montreal. If you'd like to reach me, feel free to drop me a line! I love hearing about new projects.
